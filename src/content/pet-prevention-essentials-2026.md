## How to Choose (Fast Decision Rules)

Use these three rules instead of getting lost in lists:

1. **Pick a format your schedule can support.** Monthly chewables are simple. Collars reduce reminders.  
2. **Match your region and activity level.** Outdoor dogs need stronger tick coverage.  
3. **Choose consistency over perfection.** The best product is the one you actually use on time.

## Avoid These Common Mistakes

- Skipping a dose because your schedule got busy.
- Buying a new product right before travel or boarding.
- Using multiple products without a simple monthly schedule.

Consistency protects your pet more than a perfect brand choice.
