## Fast Decision Rules

1. **Choose the format you can maintain.** Monthly chewables are simple. Collars reduce reminders.
2. **Match your region and routine.** Outdoor pets need stronger tick coverage.
3. **Consistency beats perfection.** The best product is the one you use on time.
